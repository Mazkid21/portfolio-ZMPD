About ZMPD

ZMPD Professional Development Services was founded in 2017 with a simple mission: help ski resorts solve their technology challenges with solutions built by someone who actually understands resort operations.

With 9+ years of hands-on experience at Aspen Skiing Company and Arapahoe Basin, I bring a unique perspective to every project. I've been in the trenches during holiday rushes, dealt with POS crashes on powder days, and understand why that "small" integration issue is actually costing you thousands in guest satisfaction.

While I specialize in resort technology, ZMPD also serves restaurants, retail, and other hospitality businesses. Whether it's syncing your POS with ticketing, automating locker assignments, or building custom dashboards, we focus on practical solutions that work for your team, not just on paper.

Based in Aspen, Colorado, ZMPD serves resorts and businesses across North America with remote-first development and on-site support when needed.

Recent Projects

2025 - Present

Sugar Bowl Resort - Enviso Locker System Integration

Built a production-ready automated sync between RTPOne (resort management) and Enviso (locker access control) APIs. Completely automated RFID carrier assignment and locker access management for season pass holders.

Node.js SQL Server REST APIs Clean Architecture

The Problem:

Sugar Bowl installed brand new smart lockers and wanted guests to use their existing ski passes (RFID cards) to access them. No existing integration between RTPOne and Enviso systems.

My Solution:

  • Implemented 3-phase sequential sync architecture for reliability
  • Built clean 3-layer architecture (87% code reduction from initial version)
  • Automated voucher relationship handling for family lockers
  • Created refund detection and cleanup system
  • Optimized with 4-phase reverse sync (60-80% performance improvement)

Results:

8-12 second per user sync, zero manual intervention, production-ready system awaiting October 2025 deployment when lockers are installed.
