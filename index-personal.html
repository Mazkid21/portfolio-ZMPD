About Me

I grew up in Aspen and spent most of my career in the ski industry - first at Aspen Skiing Company for 6 years, then at Arapahoe Basin as a Product Manager. Along the way, I realized I loved solving technical problems more than meetings about meetings.

So I taught myself to code (shoutout to General Assembly), started freelancing in 2017, and never looked back. Turns out, understanding how resorts actually operate makes building their software a lot easier.

These days, I mostly work on integrations - connecting POS systems to ticketing, syncing databases, building APIs. It's not glamorous, but when you save a lift ops team 40 hours a week of manual data entry, it feels pretty good.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me skiing, mountain biking, or trying to keep my houseplants alive (with mixed success).

Recent Work

2024 - Present

Sugar Bowl Resort - Locker System Integration

Built a real-time sync between their RTPOne POS and Enviso access control. What used to take staff 40+ hours per week now happens automatically.

Node.js PostgreSQL REST APIs Webhooks

The Problem:

Staff were manually entering locker assignments from the POS into the access system. During busy weekends, guests waited 20+ minutes for locker access.

My Solution:

  • Built middleware to translate between RTPOne and Enviso APIs
  • Implemented real-time webhook processing for instant updates
  • Added retry logic and error handling for network issues
  • Created admin dashboard for monitoring sync status

Results:

100% automated process, zero manual entry errors, happy guests, happier staff.
