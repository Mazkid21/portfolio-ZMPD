About ZMPD

ZMPD Professional Development Services was founded in 2017 with a simple mission: help ski resorts solve their technology challenges with solutions built by someone who actually understands resort operations.

With 9 years of hands-on experience at Aspen Skiing Company and Arapahoe Basin, I bring a unique perspective to every project. I've been in the trenches during holiday rushes, dealt with POS crashes on powder days, and understand why that "small" integration issue is actually costing you thousands in guest satisfaction.

ZMPD specializes in the unglamorous but critical work of making your existing systems talk to each other. Whether it's syncing your POS with ticketing, automating locker assignments, or building custom dashboards, we focus on practical solutions that work for your team, not just on paper.

Based in Aspen, Colorado, ZMPD serves resorts across North America with remote-first development and on-site support when needed.

ZMPD Projects

2024 - Present

Sugar Bowl Resort - Locker System Integration

Built a real-time sync between their RTPOne POS and Enviso access control. What used to take staff 40+ hours per week now happens automatically.

Node.js PostgreSQL REST APIs Webhooks

The Problem:

Staff were manually entering locker assignments from the POS into the access system. During busy weekends, guests waited 20+ minutes for locker access.

My Solution:

  • Built middleware to translate between RTPOne and Enviso APIs
  • Implemented real-time webhook processing for instant updates
  • Added retry logic and error handling for network issues
  • Created admin dashboard for monitoring sync status

Results:

100% automated process, zero manual entry errors, happy guests, happier staff.
